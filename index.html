The Vision

Addressing a clear market need with an innovative solution.

The Market Opportunity

The demand for racket sports is soaring, particularly for padel and pickleball. However, many communities lack dedicated, accessible, and modern facilities. Traditional clubs present barriers like high costs, limited hours, and inconvenient booking processes. RallyPerfect fills this gap by providing an unparalleled, flexible experience that is always available.

Our Solution: A 24/7 Automated Facility

The entire facility is built around a fully automated, staff-free model. A custom system manages court booking and facility access through a simple mobile app, keeping operational costs low and member satisfaction high.

Optimized for a Compact Footprint

Our design maximizes a limited space to provide a comprehensive sports and fitness offering. By carefully planning the layout, we can offer multiple high-demand amenities in a smaller, more cost-effective location.

The Business Model

Diversified revenue streams are integrated into a single, compact facility.

Membership Tiers

Our primary revenue source now includes access to all sports, the medium-sized gym, and the basketball court, offering unparalleled value.

Single: $29.99 / month

Couple: $49.99 / month (17% discount)

Court Rentals

Members enjoy significantly reduced rates, driving value and encouraging frequent play. Non-member rates capture casual demand.

Tennis/Padel: $20/hr prime, $10/hr off-peak

Pickleball: $10/hr prime, $5/hr off-peak

Non-Member Rate: $40/hr tennis/padel, $35/hr pickleball

Day Pass

A convenient day pass option for guests and curious potential members, offering full access to the gym and basketball court, but not to court reservations. This is a high-margin, low-effort revenue stream.

$10 / day pass

Pro Shop Vending

A fully automated, vending machine-style pro shop offers essentials like grips, balls, and paddles for a high-margin, zero-labor revenue stream.

Racquet Stringing

A secure, automated drop-off/pickup lockbox system provides a convenient, profitable service with minimal operational complexity. This service is a key value-add for serious players.

Robotic Barista

An automated barista station provides on-demand coffee, lattes, and espresso. This high-margin service enhances the member experience without increasing staffing needs.

Medium-Sized Gym

A thoughtfully-sized, 24/7 access gym with a wide range of cardio machines, free weights, and strength training equipment, now included with all membership tiers to serve members' holistic fitness needs.

Basketball Court

A dedicated half-court for pickup games and warm-ups, included in all memberships to attract a broader recreational audience.

Enforcement & Security

Our automated system ensures members-only access and facility integrity.

Automated Access Control

Each court reservation made through the mobile app generates a unique, time-sensitive access code. This code is the only way to unlock the court gates and the main facility doors, ensuring only members and their registered guests can enter during their allotted time.

Smart Monitoring

High-resolution cameras and motion sensors are strategically placed throughout the facility to deter unauthorized use. This technology not only ensures a safe environment but also provides a record of all facility access, allowing us to enforce our terms of service effectively without the need for on-site staff.
